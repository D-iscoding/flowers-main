Roses

  • Red Rose Represents deep love, passion, and romantic desire.
  • White Rose Signifies purity, innocence, and remembrance.
  • Pink Rose Represents admiration, gratitude, and sweetness.
  • Yellow Rose Symbolizes friendship, joy, and new beginnings.

Tulips

  • Red Tulip Signifies perfect love, passion, and deep feelings.
  • White Tulip Symbolizes purity, forgiveness, and a fresh start.
  • Purple Tulip Symbolizes royalty, dignity, and admiration.
  • Yellow Tulip Represents cheerful thoughts, friendship, and hope.

Daisies

  • Common Daisy Represents purity, simplicity, and new beginnings.
  • Shasta Daisy Symbolizes innocence, cheerfulness, and loyal love.
  • AfricanDaisy Represents beauty, elegance, and admiration.
